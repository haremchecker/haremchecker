This site aims to be a quick tool to check whether an anime/manga is what the owner terms a "true" harem, or a "psuedo" harem. This site passes absolutely no judgment on an individual's preferences. This is simply to help an individual find, or avoid, specific titles.

If you notice any errors, or would like to suggest a title not currently included, please contact the owner.

Many works are tagged with harem, especially on anime and manga trackers, but not all of them end up being harem. So the owner differentiates between the two.

  • A pseudo-harem is a work tagged as a harem, that may have harem-like elements. This is usually, but not limited to, multiple potential love interests for the protagonist. However, in the end, despite multiple options, the protagonist only ends up with a single person. An example of a pseudo-harem is Oregairu.
  • A true harem is when the protagonist, (usually male) ends up in a romantic/sexual relationship with some, or all, of the potential love interests. For the sake of brevity, any protagonist with more than one concurrent partner is listed in this category. An example of a true harem is High School DxD.
